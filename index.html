OpenWall
Simple social wall

Share ideas, updates, and stories in one main feed.

OpenWall is a clean social space where anyone can post, blog, and comment. Newest posts always appear at the top of the main wall.

This prototype uses local storage + Firestore.
  1. Create a demo account.
  2. Post to the main wall.
  3. Like and comment on posts.
  4. Explore topics and people.
